Christalight × Comeketo

The Signature Offer

Comeketo’s strongest fit for Christalight is a premium Brazilian BBQ experience: bold meats, elevated buffet presentation, and the option to upsell into a Gaucho / rodizio-style carving service. It feels special, photographs well, and gives Christalight a distinctive food offer that stands out from standard banquet menus.

How to Present This

Lead with Brazilian BBQ as the signature Christalight × Comeketo experience. Offer package levels first. Only if the client wants something different, move to Italian, American, Caribbean, or Latin as secondary options under the same premium catering standard.
