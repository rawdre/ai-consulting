Partnership notes

How The Partnership Could Be Framed

The core message

Garage B already has the kind of atmosphere people remember. Comeketo adds the hospitality layer — food, service style, tastings, and a culinary presentation that helps clients picture the event more fully.

The practical approach

The goal is not to make the venue generic. It is to pair Garage B’s urban identity with a food partner in a way that feels aligned, clear, and easy to present.

Partnership structure

What A Partnership Could Look Like

For the venue

  1. Comeketo could serve as a culinary partner for selected Garage B opportunities.
  2. Both sides could use a co-branded venue + catering presentation during outreach and follow-up.
  3. Interested leads could move naturally into tastings, package discussions, and planning conversations.

For the sales process

  1. Use the page in outreach, meetings, and follow-up messages when helpful.
  2. Present clear package ranges and a simple path into pricing discussions.
  3. Create a smoother transition from venue interest to food planning.
