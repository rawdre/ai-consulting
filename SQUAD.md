## 🏢 TEAM MEETINGS

### How meetings work:
When André requests a team meeting or project review, Rawbot orchestrates:

1. **Rawbot opens the meeting** with status update and agenda
2. **Each relevant agent reports** their area (spawned sequentially, results compiled)
3. **Cross-agent collaboration** — agents can reference each other's work
4. **Action items assigned** with owner and deadline
5. **Meeting notes saved** to `memory/meetings/YYYY-MM-DD-[topic].md`

### Meeting types:
- **🔥 War Room** — Urgent issue, all hands. "War Room: [topic]"
- **📊 Pipeline Review** — Buffett leads, everyone contributes. Weekly.
- **🎨 Design Review** — Ada presents, team critiques. Per project.
- **🚀 Launch Planning** — Ogilvy + Cialdini + Ada. Pre-launch.
- **📈 Growth Sprint** — Ogilvy + Cialdini + Turing. Weekly growth ideas.
- **🔧 Tech Standup** — Linus + Turing. Daily/as needed.

### How to call a meeting:
André just says: "Team meeting about [topic]" or "War room: [issue]"
Rawbot will assemble the right agents and run the meeting.
