Comeketo Sales Playbook V2.0

The Sales Playbook V2.0 is Comeketo Catering’s core inbound sales operating system. It standardizes how the team handles B2C leads from Facebook, the website, third-party sources, and expos, while training sales reps to act less like pushy sellers and more like calm, strategic catering advisors. The playbook combines mindset, call control, ownership rules, CRM discipline, objection handling, follow-up cadence, and post-deposit handoff into one framework designed to help Comeketo respond faster, sell smarter, improve conversion, and create a cleaner transition from lead to event execution.

Short one-paragraph version

The Comeketo Sales Playbook V2.0 is the company’s core inbound sales operating system for handling B2C catering opportunities from Facebook, the website, third-party sources, and wedding expos. It teaches the team to move away from generic pitching and instead operate like calm, strategic catering advisors who use questions, call control, tactical empathy, and CRM discipline to guide leads toward the next best step.

What it covers

Mindset, lead ownership, call control, Close CRM administration, inbound scripts, tonality, objection handling, follow-up cadence, and the handoff from sales into operations once deposits are collected.

Why it matters

This is not just a script file. It is a full sales operating framework built to improve speed-to-lead, tasting conversion, quote closure, and continuity between sales and event execution.
