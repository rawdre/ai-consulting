What this document is

The Big Picture

The Sales Playbook V2.0 is Comeketo Catering’s core B2C sales operating framework. It exists to standardize how inbound leads are handled from Facebook, the website, third-party platforms, and wedding expos. Its main job is to help SDRs qualify leads, book the next step with a closer, and move opportunities toward tastings, quotes, deposits, and successful event execution.

Mindset shift

The team is trained to stop acting like pushy sales reps and start acting like calm, trusted catering advisors.

Conversation control

The playbook emphasizes open-ended questions, mirroring, tactical empathy, and controlled pacing to move prospects naturally to the next step.

Operational discipline

Every lead, task, and opportunity must be tracked clearly inside Close so nothing slips through the cracks.

Core themes

What The Playbook Actually Teaches

1. Sales process

  • Respond to inbound fast
  • Book calls with closers
  • Move leads toward tastings, quotes, and deposits

2. Ownership

  • Lead owners stay accountable
  • Task owners ensure execution
  • Opportunity owners drive closure

3. CRM rigor

  • 3–5 sentence call summaries
  • Always assign next tasks
  • Keep qualification details updated

4. Call control

  • Questions steer the conversation
  • Statements kill momentum
  • Focus on gaining one next action

5. Scripting

  • Main inbound script
  • Supporting call scripts
  • Objection handling and recovery paths

6. Post-close flow

  • Collect deposit
  • Transition opportunity into operations
  • Maintain relationship continuity
Leadership read

Why This Matters

Commercially

The playbook improves speed to lead, lead handling consistency, tasting conversion, and quote closure by creating one shared method for how the team should sell.

Operationally

It reduces confusion between sales and ops by defining exactly when and how an opportunity moves from SDR/closer control into execution control after deposit collection.

Bottom line

Executive Summary

The Comeketo Sales Playbook V2.0 is not just a script document — it is a full inbound sales operating system. It combines mindset, accountability, call control, CRM standards, objection handling, follow-up cadences, and ops handoff into one framework designed to help Comeketo respond faster, sell smarter, improve conversion, and create a cleaner transition from lead generation to event execution.
