Comeketo Sales Playbook V2.0

The Comeketo Sales Playbook V2.0 is the company’s core inbound sales operating system for handling B2C catering opportunities from Facebook, the website, third-party sources, and wedding expos. It teaches the team to move away from generic pitching and instead operate like calm, strategic catering advisors who use questions, call control, tactical empathy, and CRM discipline to guide leads toward the next best step. The playbook also defines ownership across leads, tasks, and opportunities, reinforces strict standards for Close.com updates, and creates a cleaner transition from sales into operations once deposits are collected. In short, it is a full framework for helping Comeketo respond faster, sell smarter, improve conversion, and create a more reliable path from inbound lead to executed event.
