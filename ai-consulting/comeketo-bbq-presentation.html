Sales Positioning
How to Present This
Simple structure for André and Rodrigo
This final page is about the pitch. The goal is not to overwhelm the client — it is to make decision-making feel easy and premium.

1. Lead with BBQ

Present Brazilian BBQ / churrasco as the signature Christalight x Comeketo experience. It is differentiated, interactive, and feels higher value immediately.

2. Offer Package Levels

Mini, Full, Deluxe, and Supreme make pricing easier to understand. Clients should feel they are choosing between clear levels, not building from chaos.

3. Upsell the Experience

If budget allows, move them into Gaucho’s Style. That is the biggest emotional differentiator and the strongest premium move in the entire packet.
Recommended presentation flow for Jason / Christalight: “Our signature offering is Brazilian BBQ, with flexible package levels. If your client wants a different cuisine, we can also build an Italian, American, Caribbean, or Latin menu under the same premium event standard.”
